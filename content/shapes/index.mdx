# Professional Skill Shapes

Skill shapes describe the breadth and depth of an individual's professional capabilities.

## The Three Shapes

1. **I-Shaped**: Deep specialist in a single domain
2. **T-Shaped**: Deep specialist with broad complementary skills
3. **Pi-Shaped**: Multi-domain expert with deep expertise in 2+ areas
