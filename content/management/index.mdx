# Management Levels

The framework defines three distinct management layers, each with specific responsibilities.

## Three Management Layers

### Front-line Manager (Operational)
Direct people management, team execution, tactical planning.

### Middle Manager (Tactical)
Multi-team coordination, resource allocation, strategic execution.

### Top Manager (Strategic)
Organizational direction, vision setting, executive leadership.

_Detailed responsibilities and transition criteria will be added in Phase 3._
