### 3. Reference List Structure
Each reference includes:
- **Title:** Full title of article, paper, book, or resource
- **Author(s):** If available
- **Publication/Source:** Journal, blog, company, website
- **Year:** Publication year
- **Link:** Clickable URL to source material
- **Brief Description (optional):** 1-sentence summary of relevance

**Example Format:**
```
### Academic Research

1. **"The New Career Compact"**
   - Author: Corporate Research Forum
   - Source: Harvard Business Review
   - Year: 2018
   - Link: [URL]
   - Description: Explores evolving employee-employer career expectations

2. **"Career Development in Organizations"**
   - Author: Douglas T. Hall
   - Source: Journal of Applied Psychology
   - Year: 2002
   - Link: [URL]
   - Description: Foundational research on organizational career systems
```
