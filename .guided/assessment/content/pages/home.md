## Page Purpose Statement

The Home page serves as the central hub for the Career Topologies website, providing a welcoming entry point that:
1. Explains what Career Topologies is at a high level
2. Highlights the value proposition for different audiences
3. Showcases the three topology models (Y, W, Network) visually
4. Directs visitors to framework components via navigation cards
5. Establishes credibility and open-source positioning
